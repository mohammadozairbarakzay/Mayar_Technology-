Core values that drives each Laner, every day.

In our team, we trust

We strongly believe in collaborative work and focus on creating talented and committed teams where everyone has room to stand out, share their knowledge and learn from others.

Proud to be techie

We are curious and strive to learn continuously. Challenges are our motors. We believe in the importance of listening to the professional goals of each member and doing our best to make them happen.

It’s up to you

We are promoters of autonomous work. People can organize their work with flexibility and choose freely where to do it from (we are totally decentralized!) but always honoring the commitments they made with the project and the team.

Leave you mark

We want you to be a key player, challenge what is necessary, think outside the box, find new solutions, and propose the improvements that are to be made. We are always open to suggestions, and we value people who show initiative.

Keep it simple

We act with agility to achieve our goals. Bureaucracy bores us; we seek to be more practical and determined to make things happen and meet the needs of our people and our clients at the right time.

Recruiting Process

The interview process is, in most cases, standard across the industry, but it’s always nice to understand what you are going to face and the different steps we have.

Talent Acquisition Interview

The initial meeting is where we tell you about our team and our history, culture, and the challenge we have for you. We will get to know each other and answer any questions you have.

Technical Interview

You’ll meet one of our engineers who will evaluate your skills and knowledge of the required technology for the position.

Client Interview

You will be introduced to the project leader to get to know each other, and where you will talk about your experiences as a developer.

Welcome to the Brand

You will get your formal offer and be presented to the company in our internal channels. We'll book your first meetings and start your onboarding process.
