Data Processing ▲
Extract of data that comes from stream or batch sources and subsequently cleanse it to make it available on database or cloud systems, data scientists or any user that to wants to utilize the data.
Data Storage & ETL ▲
Extract, process and transform your data and load it into relational, non-relational, noSQL, cloud or datawarehouse systems based on the type of data your are handling that will allow you to analyze information more efficiently.
Data Pipelines ▲
Develop data workflow pipelines to Integrate all your sources of data and store all of them in one place, in order to process it in batchs and gain business insights for competitive advantages.
Data Lakes ▲
Develop and generate a repository of raw data that is kept there for as long as necessary and is ready to be analyzed by experts.
Data Mining ▲
Work with your own data to establish trends, hidden patterns, anomalies and correlations that can help you predict the outcome.
Reporting & Processing ▲
Automate your reports based on your data that will allow you to evaluate the result (for better or worse) of the action you are carrying out.
